---
title: Chat Rules
---
These rules apply both in-game and in the Discord server.

**Treat everyone with respect.** Harassment, witch hunting, sexism, racism, homophobia, and/or any other sort of discrimination/bigotry/hate speech will not be tolerated.

**No spam or self-promotion** (server invites, advertisements, etc) without permission from a staff member. This includes DMing fellow members.

**No age-restricted or obscene content.** This includes text, images, or links featuring nudity, sex, hard violence, or other graphically disturbing content.

We reserve the right to take moderation actions against you for any reason, _including_ those not stated in these rules.

And, remember: **if you see something against the rules or something that makes you feel unsafe, feel free to let staff know.** We want this server to be a welcoming space!
