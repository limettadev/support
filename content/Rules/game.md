The [Chat Rules](chat.md) apply in-game too.

We reserve the right to take moderation actions against you for any reason, including those not stated in these rules.

> [!INFO] Please note:
> Some rules do not apply to all matches. When playing, please use the **`/uhc`** command to take a look at the match rules and see what is and isn't allowed.
## Bans

The following can and will get you banned:
- **Any sort of cheats** - Don't use hacked clients or any mods that give you an unfair advantage.
- **X-Ray / Cavefinder** - Don't use a mod or resource pack to help you find ores or caves easily.
- **Griefing your Team** - Don't throw/grief in team games! This includes killing yourself and leaving because you don't like your random teammate.
- **DDoS-ing** - Don't attempt to or threaten to boot the server offline.
- **iPvP** - Don't attempt to damage other players before PvP is enabled.
- **Stalking** - Don't continue to stalk/kill a player if you were warned not to.
- **Bug/Glitch Abuse** - Don't abuse bugs/glitches to gain an unfair advantage.
- **Extreme Spoiling** - Don't spoil crucial information (for example, moles in a game).
- **Crossteaming** - Don't team with other teams (or with other players in FFA).
- **Unfair Advantage** - If your teammate is breaking the rules and you're knowingly benefiting from it, you will also be punished.
## Warnings

The following will get you a warning at first, but if continued can still get you a kick/ban!
- **Prebuilding** - In SkyHigh games, your base shouldn't be less than 20 blocks from another team/player's base.
- **Groundcamping** - In SkyHigh games, don't camp on the ground waiting for players.
- **Skybasing/Towering** - Don't make skybases/towers during meetup.
- **Ignoring meetup** - During meetup, you should go to 0, 0! This applies even if you were busy doing something, like enchanting, shearing, mining, etc...
- **Running at Meetup** - Don't run away from multiple fights during meetup. This rule is typically loose depending on the host.
- **Teaming in Arena** - Don't crossteam with other players in the FFA Arena.
